About GiftMate

GiftMate is the perfect way to make your holiday gift-giving easy and fun! With our tool, simply input the names of the participants and let the tool randomly assign gift recipients. Make your gift-giving assignment hassle free in easy 3 steps:

Step 1. Input all participants and describe the lottery you want to run.

Step 2. Share with all the participants one time using only a link that was generated for them.

Step 3. Participants will visit the one time link which will reveal to them who they will have to gift.
